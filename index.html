About Veltra

A residential developer built on craft, partnership and the long view.

Veltra was founded by four industry veterans who, between them, have spent their careers turning raw land into thriving streets. We deliver considered residential developments — single homes, duplexes, townhomes and master-planned communities — with the discipline of seasoned operators and the care of people building somewhere they'd live themselves.

The name says it: Velocity + extra. We move quickly because we know what to do — and we add the extra because it's what turns a project into a home.

How we work

Four founders. One project lead. No hand-offs.

  1. 01

    Listen first

    We start by understanding the land, the brief and what success actually looks like for you. No pitch decks, no pressure.

  2. 02

    Design with discipline

    Our design partners are chosen for craft, not headlines. Every plan is stress-tested for buildability and budget before it leaves the table.

  3. 03

    Build accountably

    A founder owns each project end-to-end. You always know who's responsible — because they're the one in the meeting.

  4. 04

    Hand over with pride

    We don't disappear at handover. The homes we build carry the Veltra name, and we stand behind them.
